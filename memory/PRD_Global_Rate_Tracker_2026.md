# PRD: 2026 Global Interest Rate Tracker & Mortgage Impact Calculator

## 1. Executive Summary
In February 2026, the global macroeconomic landscape is characterized by cautious stability in Europe and the UK, and high seasonal urgency in Canada. This product aims to provide homeowners and investors with a unified dashboard to track central bank movements against market rates, specifically addressing the February/March seasonal pivot points like the UK lending volume surge and the Canadian RRSP deadline.

## 2. Target Markets
- **UK:** Homeowners facing a 3.75% BoE plateau and shifting lending conditions.
- **Canada:** Tax-conscious investors and mortgage holders approaching the March 2nd RRSP deadline.
- **EU:** Consumers looking for long-term recovery signals amid ECB holds.
- **US:** Global investors tracking cross-border rate differentials.

## 3. Core Features
### 3.1. Real-Time Rate Comparison Engine
- **Central Bank vs. Market Rates:** A side-by-side visualization of official policy rates (BoE, ECB, BoC, Fed) against actual retail mortgage and lending rates.
- **Spread Tracker:** Monitoring the "margin" banks are taking over central bank rates.

### 3.2. Canada-Specific: "RRSP Deadline" Contribution Impact Module
- **Deadline:** March 2, 2026.
- **Functionality:** Calculates the tax refund impact of RRSP contributions and how that refund can be applied to accelerate mortgage principal payments (Lump sum impact analysis).
- **SEO Hook:** "CRA RRSP Deadline 2026 Strategy".

### 3.3. Amortization Sensitivity Analysis (UK & EU Focus)
- **Scenario Modeling:** Users can simulate a +/- 0.25% or 0.50% shift in BoE/ECB rates to see the immediate impact on their monthly mortgage payments and total interest paid over the term.
- **BoE Context:** Tailored for the current 5-4 split in the MPC, showing "Hawkish" vs "Dovish" repayment futures.

### 3.4. Global Lending Forecast Dashboard
- Visualizes the 4% forecast rise in UK lending volumes for 2026.
- Tracks the EU consumer recovery thesis progress.

## 4. Technical Requirements
- **Data Ingestion:** Real-time APIs for central bank rates and scraping/feed integration for commercial bank mortgage rates.
- **Localization:** Multi-currency support (GBP, CAD, EUR, USD) and regional tax logic for Canada.

## 5. SEO & Growth Strategy
### 5.1. Primary Keywords
- **"BoE Rate Forecast 2026":** Content focus on the 5-4 MPC split and its implications for fixed-rate mortgage renewals.
- **"CRA RRSP Deadline 2026 Strategy":** Tool-driven content explaining how to use the RRSP refund for mortgage deleveraging.

### 5.2. Content Pillars
- **Weekly Rate Pulse:** Commentary on the "5-4 Split" and what it means for the next BoE meeting.
- **The March 2nd Countdown:** Daily tips for Canadian users on maximizing RRSP contributions before the deadline.

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Engagement:** Number of "Sensitivity Simulations" run per user.
- **Conversion:** Click-through rate to partner mortgage brokers or financial advisors.
- **SEO Rank:** Top 3 position for "BoE Rate Forecast 2026" within 30 days.

## 7. Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Immediate):** Launch Canadian RRSP Calculator (due to March 2nd deadline).
- **Phase 2 (Mid-March):** Launch UK/EU Amortization Sensitivity tools.
- **Phase 3 (April):** Full Global Comparison Dashboard.
