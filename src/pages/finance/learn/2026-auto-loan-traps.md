In 2026, the average car price is still hovering near record highs. To make the monthly payment "fit," dealers are pushing 7-year (84-month) loans. 

## The Negative Equity Trap
Cars depreciate. After 3 years, you'll likely owe more than the car is worth. If you crash it or need to sell it, you're stuck. Run the math on our [EMI Calculator](/finance/emi) and look at the "Total Interest Paid" field. It's sickening.

## The 4-20-10 Rule
4 years max, 20% down, 10% of monthly income. If you can't hit that, you can't afford the car...
