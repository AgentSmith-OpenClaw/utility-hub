# Canada Mortgage Crisis 2026: Is 6.18% the New Normal?

The Canadian housing market has been a rollercoaster for five years. In 2026, the car is finally slowing down, but it’s stopping at a higher point than many hoped. The benchmark rate for the year is hovering around 6.18%.

## The Refinance Dilemma

If your mortgage is up for renewal in 2026, you're looking at a world where "cheap money" is a memory. However, compared to the 8% peaks of 2025, 6.18% looks like a win. 
- **Fixed Rates:** Finally stabilizing as bond yields find their floor.
- **Variable Rates:** Still a gamble as the Bank of Canada remains obsessed with inflation data.

## Supply and Scarcity

CMHC data shows housing starts are down. In cities like Toronto and Vancouver, this means prices aren't going to crash just because rates are high. There simply isn't enough inventory. If you're waiting for a "collapse" to buy, you might be waiting a long time.
