# Fixed vs. Variable: Understanding UK Mortgage Rates in 2026

The UK mortgage market in early 2026 is in a tough spot. With the Bank of England maintaining a cautious stance on interest rates despite falling inflation, homeowners are faced with a difficult choice: lock in a fixed rate now or gamble on a tracker.

## Current Market Market
As of February 2025, the average 2-year fixed mortgage rate has stabilized around 4.5%, while 5-year fixes are slightly more competitive at 4.2%. However, the underlying volatility in the Gilt markets means these rates can shift overnight.

## Why Fixing Might Make Sense
For those prioritizing budget certainty, a fixed-rate mortgage remains the gold standard. In an era of "higher for longer" expectations, locking in a rate below 4.5% provides a hedge against potential geopolitical shocks that could reignite inflation.
