## Roth vs. Traditional: The 2026 Dilemma

With the 2026 tax sunset potentially raising tax brackets for many Americans (as discussed in our [US Tax Planning Guide](https://toolisk.com/finance/learn/us-tax-planning-2026)), the choice between a Traditional 401(k) and a Roth 401(k) has never been more critical.

**Traditional 401(k):** Provides an immediate tax break now. If you believe your tax rate will be lower in retirement than it is in 2026, this remains the gold standard.

**Roth 401(k):** You pay taxes on the money now, but withdrawals in retirement are tax-free. In a rising tax environment, locking in today's rates can be a brilliant long-term move.
