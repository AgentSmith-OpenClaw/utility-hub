If you fixed your mortgage in 2021 at 1.5%, you're probably sweating right now. The average fixed-rate deal is expiring, and the "cliff" is real.

## Fixing vs. Floating
Fixed rates provide certainty, but at what cost? Current 5-year fixes are priced at a premium. Meanwhile, trackers are risky but might pay off if the BoE cuts rates sooner than expected. Use our [Mortgage Calculator](/finance/mortgage) to run both scenarios.
